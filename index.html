Crafting Reimagined, Community Redefined

The crafting community you need to level up your fiber game.

Fiber friends is the ultimate crafting companion. With Fiber Friends, discover an all-in-one crafting club that consolidates a diverse library of knitting and crocheting patterns, step-by-step tutorials, and a vibrant, supportive community. Whether you're a beginner seeking guidance, an intermediate crafter looking to expand your skills, or an experienced artist eager to share your expertise, Fiber Friends is your creative haven. Uniting the world of crafting in one convenient platform, we empower you to craft with confidence, connect meaningfully with fellow enthusiasts, and elevate your crafting journey. Join us, and let's make crafting accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone.

Custom For You Page

Your personalized for you page allows you to effortlessly scroll to find the designs that inspire you to create. Save your favorite designs to your personal collections page. You can organize you collections so that all your patterns are easy to find.

Mark Up Patterns!

Use our editing tools to annotate your patterns, strikethrough steps you’ve already completed, highlight important notes, and post tabs so you always know where you left off!

Connect With Others

Fiber friends allows you to cultivate meaningful connections with other crafters just like you! Send direct messages and share posts with your friends! Have a question? Join a chatroom! Fiber Friends has a chat room for everyone. If you get stuck, need to troubleshoot, or just want to know more about a topic, there’s a chat room for you!

Show and Tell!

Fiber Friends enables you to post with two specific intentions: Brag about your latest creation! We want you to post about projects you are proud of and allows the community to hype you up! Post a pattern! Have a tutorial and pattern to share with the community? Amazing, we want to know! Fiber Friends encourages community contributions.

Stitch Counter

Fiber Friends has designed a mobile application for your apple watch so that you can keep track of all your stitches and rows for every project! It allows you to store multiple projects at once and makes it possible for you to pause and restart counting to increase convenience for you!
